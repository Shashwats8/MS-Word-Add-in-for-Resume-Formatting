# MS-Word-Add-in-for-Resume-Formatting

Reume formatting is widely used in the us staffing Industry and it is widely used in the HR Tech Industries. This add-in will be helping the recruiters to optimise the workflow and help them with creating formats of the specific documents and that document will get formatted in the way in which user wanted to make it in. 

Resume formatting plays a critical role in the US staffing industry and is highly relevant in HR Tech ecosystems. Recruiters often need to standardize resumes for client submissions, ensuring consistency and professionalism.

This add-in will help recruiters optimize their workflow by automatically generating and formatting documents based on predefined or user-specific templates. It enables faster turnaround, reduces manual effort, and ensures that resumes are structured according to client or organizational requirements.
